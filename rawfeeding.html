Raw Feeding for Dogs

Raw feeding is a beneficial way to provide your dog with a natural and nutritious diet. It involves feeding your dog a variety of raw foods, including muscle meat, raw meaty bones, liver, secreting organs, and sometimes fruits and vegetables.

What is a Raw Food Diet?

A raw food diet for dogs consists of:

  • Muscle meat, providing essential proteins
  • Raw meaty bones, promoting dental health and providing calcium
  • Liver, a rich source of vitamins and minerals
  • Secreting organs, such as kidneys and spleen, for additional nutrients
  • Occasional fruits and vegetables to add variety and essential nutrients

Benefits of Raw Feeding

  • Promotes healthy digestion
  • Improves coat and skin health
  • Boosts energy levels
  • Supports dental health
  • Reduces risk of allergies and sensitivities
  • Enhances overall well-being and longevity
