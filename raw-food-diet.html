Raw Feeding for Dogs

What is a Raw Food Diet?

A raw food diet for dogs consists of a variety of raw foods, each providing essential nutrients for your dog's health:

  • Muscle meat: Provides essential proteins
  • Raw meaty bones: Promotes dental health and provides calcium
  • Liver: A rich source of vitamins and minerals
  • Secreting organs: Such as kidneys and spleen, for additional nutrients
  • Occasional fruits and vegetables: Adds variety and essential nutrients

Benefits of Raw Feeding

  • Promotes healthy digestion
  • Improves coat and skin health
  • Boosts energy levels
  • Supports dental health
  • Reduces risk of allergies and sensitivities
  • Enhances overall well-being and longevity
